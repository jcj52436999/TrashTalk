Trash Talk Questionaire


TRASHTALK brings together community members looking to clean their neighborhoods helps neighbors organize in their Neighborhoods. Enter your address and times when you are available to volunteer. TrashTalk will show you clean-ups available in your area. If there are no clean-ups, TrashTalk will provide a list of dumping sites where you can start a clean-up. Finally, if you know of a dumping site that is not listed, you can add it to the list.


Your message will be sent to people who are interested in cleaning a specific point.
